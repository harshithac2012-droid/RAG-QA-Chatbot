About RAG Technology

Understanding Retrieval-Augmented Generation

What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an advanced AI technique that combines the power of large language models with external knowledge retrieval. This hybrid approach enables AI systems to provide more accurate, up-to-date, and contextually relevant responses by grounding answers in specific document content.

How RAG Chatbot Works

1
Document Ingestion & Processing
Your PDF document is uploaded and parsed. The system extracts all text content and breaks it down into smaller, meaningful chunks (typically paragraphs or sections) to make processing more efficient.
2
Vector Embedding Creation
Each text chunk is converted into a mathematical representation called a vector embedding. These embeddings capture the semantic meaning of the text, allowing the system to understand context and relationships between different parts of your document.
3
Vector Storage
The embeddings are stored in a specialized vector database that enables fast similarity searches. This database is optimized for finding relevant information quickly, even across large documents.
4
Query Processing
When you ask a question, your query is also converted into a vector embedding using the same process, ensuring compatibility with the stored document embeddings.
5
Semantic Search & Retrieval
The system performs a similarity search in the vector database to find the most relevant chunks from your document that match your question. This goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand the actual meaning and context.
6
Context Assembly
The top matching chunks are retrieved and assembled to provide comprehensive context for your question. This ensures the AI has all relevant information needed to generate an accurate answer.
7
Answer Generation
The retrieved context is fed to a large language model along with your original question. The AI generates a comprehensive, accurate answer that's grounded in your document's actual content, significantly reducing hallucinations and ensuring factual accuracy.

Key Benefits

  • Provides accurate answers grounded in your specific documents
  • Reduces AI hallucinations by using real document content
  • Enables efficient question-answering on large documents
  • Maintains context and relevance throughout conversations
  • Scales effectively for enterprise document processing
